About The Family Group

Fellow Weddou is a family group. This family group is established to pursue the following objectives: • To promote, encourage and develop the family group. • Promoting and maintaining the highest standards of respect in the operational activities of the family group. • Upholding the rules of the family group. • Mobilising all members of the family group. • Providing equal opportunities for successful participation by all members.
